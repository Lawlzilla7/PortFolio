Dungeon and death

Dungeon and Death is a game jam game that a couple of friends and I made. The theme of the jam was 'Sacrifices must be made'. The goal of the game is to survive the dungeon, reach the end of each stage, and descend to the next level. To align with the jam's theme, we came up with the idea that your three starting companions all have different needs, such as food, and when their resources run out, they die. However, there isn't enough food for all your companions, so you must make decisions and let go of some as you progress.

For this project, I didn't do much programming because I wanted to focus more on 3D modeling, which was something I wanted to learn, especially texturing the models. I used MagicaVoxel for the 3D models and textures. I chose that program because it's ideal for creating quick and visually appealing models, and it allows you to paint directly on the models. Additionally, I handled all the 2D sprites and animations. I used Pyxel Edit for the sprites because its built-in animation tools are ideal for games.
